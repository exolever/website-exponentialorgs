About the Book

After the best-selling book “Exponential Organizations: Why new organizations are ten times better, faster, and cheaper than yours (and what to do about it)”, organizations all around the world are trying to implement the ExO model. ExO is considered the new innovation model that allowing organizations to adapt themselves for the Fourth Industrial Revolution. This new book, “Exponential Organization Transformation” describes HOW to implement the ExO model by following a proven process called the ExO Sprint – using specific tools like the ExO Canvas. The authors of the book have successfully run these processes and used these tools in dozens of organizations around the world proving that the model really works.
